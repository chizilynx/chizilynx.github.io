  • Internet and hotspot subscriptions are non refundable once activated.
  • Refunds only apply if the company completely fails to deliver service due to reasons within its control.

  • Equipment is refundable only if unused, sealed, and returned within 48 hours.
  • Once installed or powered, the equipment becomes non refundable.

6. Hotspot Vouchers and Data Packages

  • Vouchers and time based access are non refundable once issued.
  • Validity may be extended only if the service disruption was caused by the company.

7. Installation Fees

Installation, configuration, and site survey fees are non refundable once performed.

8. No Refund for Third Party Failures

No refunds are allowed due to telecom outages, national internet issues, client device problems, or environmental interference.

9. Processing of Refunds

Approved refunds are processed within 14 business days via bank transfer or mobile money.
